# QuranOn - Comprehensive Information Database

## Overview

**QuranOn** is a modern, responsive web application designed for online Quran education, connecting students worldwide with certified Quran teachers through personalized learning experiences.

## Mission & Vision

### Mission
To provide accessible, high-quality Quran education to Muslim families worldwide through innovative technology and personalized learning approaches.

### Vision
To become the leading global platform for online Quranic education, combining traditional Islamic teaching methods with modern educational technology.

## Platform Details

### Website Structure
- **Domain**: QuranOn platform (deployed on Firebase Hosting)
- **Architecture**: Single Page Application (SPA) built with React
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Language**: Primary English interface with multilingual teacher support

### Core Educational Services

#### 1. Live One-on-One Classes
- **Format**: Real-time video sessions between student and teacher
- **Duration**: Typically 30-60 minutes per session
- **Frequency**: Flexible scheduling (2-5 classes per week recommended)
- **Technology**: Video conferencing with screen sharing capabilities
- **Tools**: Digital whiteboard for interactive learning

#### 2. Learning Paths Offered

##### Quran Reading (Qaida)
- **Target Audience**: Beginners (children and adults)
- **Curriculum**: Arabic alphabet recognition, pronunciation, basic reading
- **Duration**: 3-6 months for completion
- **Outcome**: Ability to read Quran with basic Tajweed

##### Quran Memorization (Hifz)
- **Levels**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
- **Methodology**: Progressive memorization with revision
- **Tracking**: Daily, weekly, and monthly progress monitoring
- **Completion**: Full Quran memorization certification

##### Tajweed Courses
- **Focus**: Proper pronunciation and recitation rules
- **Topics**: Makharij (articulation points), Sifaat (attributes), Rules of Noon and Meem
- **Levels**: Basic to Advanced Tajweed
- **Certification**: Tajweed completion certificate

##### Islamic Values
- **Content**: Islamic manners, daily duas, stories of prophets
- **Age Groups**: Tailored for children (5-12) and teens (13-18)
- **Integration**: Combined with Quran learning
